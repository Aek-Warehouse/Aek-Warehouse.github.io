Gibson Capital LLC · 2025–Present

Financial modeling and retirement analysis.

My work focuses on developing retirement simulations, organizing their results, and presenting complex financial projections in a form that is easier to understand.

Developing the model

I created a Monte Carlo retirement simulator in R that allows users to adjust 14 financial inputs, including withdrawal limits, additional cash inflows, inflation, investment returns, and planning timeframes.

Analyzing the results

The model calculates outcomes such as probability of success, median and average ending portfolio values, and the amount of time a portfolio remains above its initial spending level.

Presenting the information

I designed graphs and summary tables that turn repeated simulation results into clear projections that can be used during retirement-planning conversations.

Professional application

The tool was developed for Gibson Capital, a financial advisory firm managing more than $2.5 billion in client assets. I also help communicate model results and am contributing to a financial paper based on the project.
