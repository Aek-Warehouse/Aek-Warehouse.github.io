Retirement Monte Carlo Simulator.

An R-based retirement planning model that simulates portfolio outcomes across changing withdrawal strategies, market returns, inflation, and other financial assumptions.

Modeling retirement outcomes.

I built this simulator to test how different spending decisions and market conditions affect a retirement portfolio over time. It generates probability-of-success estimates, portfolio projections, and summary tables that make the results easier to evaluate.
